Over The Beerbox

About Us

Welcome to GEBEYA, where convenience meets quality in every bite and sip! We are passionate about bringing delicious food and refreshing drinks right to your doorstep, ensuring you enjoy a seamless and satisfying experience from browsing to delivery.

Our mission is simple: to offer a wide range of food and drink options that cater to every taste, craving, and occasion. Whether you're looking for a healthy snack, a gourmet meal, a refreshing soda, or a premium craft beverage, we’ve got you covered with a curated selection of both soft and alcoholic drinks, as well as a variety of freshly made food items.

Zijn de bieren eenmaal uit het assortiment? Dan moet je geluk hebben dat we ze nogmaals aanbieden, maar voor iedereen kunnen we natuurlijk een uitzondering maken.

Why Choose Us?

Quality Assurance: We handpick each item to ensure it meets our rigorous quality standards.

Diverse Selection: With options ranging from light snacks to gourmet meals, and a variety of beverages, there's something for everyone.

Fast & Reliable Delivery: We prioritize timely and safe deliveries to make sure your order arrives fresh and ready to enjoy.

Customer-Centric Service: Our support team is here to assist you every step of the way, ensuring a smooth and satisfying experience.

Thank you for choosing GEBEYA for your food and drink needs. We’re excited to serve you, and we look forward to making every order memorable!
